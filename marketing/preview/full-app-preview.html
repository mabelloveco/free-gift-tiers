Progress Bar Theme Block

Add a progress bar to your theme that shows customers how close they are to receiving their free gift.

Installation Steps

  1. Go to your Shopify Admin → Online Store → Themes
  2. Click "Customize" on your active theme
  3. Navigate to the page where you want to add the progress bar (e.g., Cart page)
  4. Click "Add block" and search for "gift-progress-motivators"
  5. Add the "Gift Progress Motivators" block to your desired section
  6. Save your changes

The app template comes with an additional page which demonstrates how to create multiple pages within app navigation using App Bridge.
