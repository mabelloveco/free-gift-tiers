Campaign Configuration

How it works

When customers add items to their cart that meet or exceed your threshold, they'll automatically receive the specified gift product for free.

The campaign runs automatically through Shopify Functions and doesn't require any additional setup once configured.

Theme Integration

Progress Bar Theme Block

Add a progress bar to your theme that shows customers how close they are to receiving their free gift. This block automatically reads your campaign configuration and displays the current progress.

Installation Steps

  1. Go to your Shopify Admin → Online Store → Themes
  2. Click "Customize" on your active theme
  3. Navigate to the page where you want to add the progress bar (e.g., Cart page)
  4. Click "Add block" and search for "gift-progress-motivators"
  5. Add the "Gift Progress Motivators" block to your desired section
  6. Save your changes

How it works

The progress bar block automatically:

  • Reads your campaign configuration from the gift_tiers.config metafield
  • Shows the current cart total and remaining amount needed
  • Displays the gift product that will be added
  • Updates in real-time as customers add items to their cart
💡 Pro tip: Place the progress bar on your cart page or product pages to encourage customers to reach the free gift threshold.

Need help?

If you're having trouble with the theme integration, make sure:

  • Your campaign is configured and enabled
  • The gift variant ID is correct
  • Your theme supports app blocks
